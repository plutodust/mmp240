The Mandalorian is set after the fall of the Empire and before the emergence of the First Order. We follow the travails of a lone gunfighter in the outer reaches of the galaxy, far from the authority of the New Republic.

Episodes

Season 1

Chapter 1: The Mandalorian

In the lawless aftermath of the collapse of the Galactic Empire, an armored bounty hunter takes on a well-paying yet cryptic assignment.

Chapter 2: The Child

Having tracked down a valuable quarry, the Mandalorian is left stranded after a thieving band of scavengers strips his ship; to get off the planet, he is forced to broker an even more dangerous deal.

Chapter 3: The Sin

The battered Mandalorian returns to his client to collect his reward and tend to his wounds; complications arise and what should have been a simple task becomes far more dangerous than anyone could have expected.

Chapter 4: Sanctuary

After a narrow escape, the Mandalorian lies low on a remote planet where he meets an ex-soldier and the two of them are approached by farmers to help defend their village.

Chapter 5: The Gunslinger

The Mandalorian crosses paths with a rookie bounty hunter who is looking to bring in a quarry that is well beyond his skill, so he asks the Mandalorian for help.

Chapter 6:The Prisoner

The Mandalorian joins a mismatched crew of cutthroat mercenaries trying to spring a convict from a prison ship, a mission that is dangerous and more difficult than it first appeared.

Chapter 7: The Reckoning

An old rival extends an invitation for the Mandalorian to make peace, and hoping he may be able to resolve his disputes without more bloodshed, the Mandalorian decides to end the conflict once and for all.

Chapter 8: Redemption

The Mandalorian is cornered by powerful enemies, including one who knows all about his past and is out to ensure he has no future.
